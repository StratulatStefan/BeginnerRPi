Raspberry Pi 4 Model B/4GB

- Procesor Broadcom 2711, pe arhitectura Cortex - A72, 64 biti, 4 nuclee fizie si frecventa 1.5 GHz

- Memorie RAM 4GB LPDDR4 SDRAM

- WiFi : 2.4/5 GHz IEE 802.11 b/g/nac

- Bluetooth : 5.0 BLE

- Ethernet : Gigabit

- Header GPIO cu 40 pini

- H.265 decode (4kp60) & H.264 decode (1080p60) & H.264 encode (1080p30)
